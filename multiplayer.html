THE RULES

  • Player One goes first. Roll the dice to begin the game.
  • The dice appears on the first roll.
  • Each time the dice is rolled the score adds up.
  • You can choose to hold if you hit a ONE without holding you lose your score.
  • If one players score passes 50, you win.
  • If you roll a 1 at any time, it will become your opponents turn.
  • When you're ready click the roll button.
